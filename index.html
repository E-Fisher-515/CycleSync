Insights & Education

Why your appetite feels high today

During the luteal phase, progesterone increases, which can lead to increased hunger and cravings. This is completely normal!

Fasting Safety in PCOS

If you have PCOS, consider shorter fasting windows (12-14 hours) to maintain stable blood sugar levels.

Cycle-Aware Nutrition

Focus on iron-rich foods during menstruation and complex carbs during the luteal phase for better energy.
